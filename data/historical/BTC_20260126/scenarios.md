## Key Learnings from This Session

1. **Volume confirms moves**: The major breakout (Scenario 3) had 3-10x normal volume. The crash (Scenario 5) also showed volume spike.

2. **Exhaustion signals**: The session high was followed by 25 minutes of consolidation (Scenario 4) before the reversal. This distribution pattern is a warning sign.

3. **V-shaped recovery**: After the crash to session low, the recovery was strong and sustained. Buying at extreme lows after capitulation volume can be highly profitable.

4. **Pump and dump pattern**: This session showed classic "pump and dump" structure - aggressive buying followed by equally aggressive selling. The net result was a slight gain (close > open) but with massive volatility in between.
